DIY Science: Make Your Own Lava Lamp

Posted on: April 21, 2025

Want to create something fun, colorful, and science-y right at home? Try making your own lava lamp with a few simple supplies!

What You Need:

  • A clear plastic bottle
  • Vegetable oil
  • Water
  • Food coloring
  • Alka-Seltzer tablets (or any fizzing tablets)

Steps:

  1. Fill the bottle 2/3 full with vegetable oil.
  2. Fill the rest with water, but leave a little space at the top.
  3. Add a few drops of food coloring (it will mix with the water).
  4. Break an Alka-Seltzer tablet into pieces and drop one in.
  5. Watch the bubbly lava magic happen!
