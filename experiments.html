🧪 Experiment of the Week

Magic Milk Explosion

This colorful experiment shows how soap breaks surface tension. When you add dish soap to milk with food coloring, the colors swirl and dance in a burst of motion!

What You Need: Milk, dish soap, food coloring, a cotton swab, and a shallow dish.

Try it! Pour milk in a plate, add drops of food coloring, then dip a soapy cotton swab in — watch the explosion of color!
